Life on Earth began at least 3.5 billion years ago
Since then, life has come a long
and complex way to become
the way it is today. The 26 articles
of the History of Life series
will take you on a journey to explore
the stages through which life evolved on our planet.
This series focuses on four aeons to discuss
all of the important events that changed life
on our planet. Starting in the time when Earth
was lifeless, following through first one-celled
organisms, mass extinctions, to first mammals
and humans, History of Life On Earth
is a detailed series about past and present
life on Earth.
